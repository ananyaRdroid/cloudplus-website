GRC Services

Introduction

Helping the organization achieve an optimum balance of cost of assurance with the cost of risk materializing. Irrespective of scale and size of the organization, it is subject to several types of risks. As the organization gets larger, the cost associated with these risks increases proportionately. In SME-s as well as corporate sector, to manage risks effectively, it is necessary to have a vision backed by institutionalized processes in the GRC space. Awareness is another key topic in this space. It is necessary to keep the organization in tune to the continuous changes to the opportunities and challenges landscape. This calls for a mature framework for awareness. Our goal is to work with the key stakeholders in organizations to conceive a GRC vision and to work with the operations team to craft processes to deliver to this vision. We help organizations ensure that operations risks related to the organization are identified correctly and managed adequately be they in the space of Compliance, Information Security, Business Continuity & DR or Quality.

Our Approach

Cloudplus approaches GRC in three stages, viz Consulting / Assessment, Implementation and Sustenance. We design the right solutions which cover the risks to our customers, considering the aspects of flexibility and agility required to be successful in today's markets.

Guaranteed Profit Strategies

Effective GRC implementation helps the organization to ensure that risks related to Compliance, Information Security, Business Continuity etc. are mitigated in timely manner and improve the organizational control of Information Security and Compliance through an integrated approach. GRC can help businesses align IT to business goals, manage risk effectively and stay focussed on compliance.

    Ensuring that the key operations risks in the organization are identified at various levels and effectively managed.
    Helping the organization achieve an optimum balance of cost of assurance with the cost of risk materializing.
    Identification of areas of improvement in the GRC space and assisting the organization address these.
    Help the organization design and implement mechanisms to ensure that risks related to Compliance, Information Security, Business Continuity etc. are mitigated.
    High level exercises to assess the strengths and areas of improvements for the organization in the following areas.
